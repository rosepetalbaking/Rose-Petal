About Sugar Petal

Home-based online bakery • Serving Harrisonburg, Virginia • Local delivery only

At Sugar Petal, we create handmade desserts inspired by Middle Eastern flavors and traditions, blended with a modern, delicate touch. Every treat is baked in small batches using quality ingredients and traditional techniques — never mass-produced.

From fragrant pastries infused with rose and pistachio to comforting classic desserts, everything is made to order with care and heart.
