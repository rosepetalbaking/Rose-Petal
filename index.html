About Sugar Petal

Sugar Petal is a home-based bakery specializing in handmade Middle Eastern–inspired desserts, crafted in small batches with care and tradition. Each item is made to order using quality ingredients and classic techniques.

We offer local delivery only to ensure freshness and attention to detail from our kitchen to your door.

Orders must be placed at least 48 hours in advance.
All sales are final. Payments are non-refundable once an order is confirmed.
